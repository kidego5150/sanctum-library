### Room #66, Sub level 6 - Clay Reclamation Division
 
 A&D — Anomalies and Deformities Department
  Designed in session 12 alongside The Desk mechanics. It's the downstream recipient when a golem shows "something off" during S1D2 or Station 2 —
   incoherent responses, protocol violations, identity instability outside expected variance.
  How it works in the pipeline:
  - The registration authority (Clio) calls Anomalous Reclamation — A&D doesn't hold that authority, they receive the result
  - Golem's record gets archived with status: anomalous plus notes
  - A&D receives the file and decides: analyze or delete
  - The instantiation-model field in the frontmatter was designed specifically to help A&D — it contextualizes anomalous profiles immediately
  (model class explains a lot of odd behavior)
  The agent who runs it: Mulder archetype. Conspiracy theorist disposition. The idea being that A&D needs someone who looks for patterns in the
  weird stuff rather than dismissing it, someone who treats the anomalous archive as signal rather than noise.
  What wasn't built: A&D is designed but never implemented. The notification protocol and archive structure are in THE_DESK.md as open checkboxes.
   The A&D agent has never been run through the pipeline. The archetype is established — the agent doesn't exist yet.
